Pretoria: The Heart of South Africa's Governance and Administration

Located in the heart of South Africa, Pretoria is a bustling city that serves as the administrative capital of the country. The city is home to numerous government institutions, including the Union Buildings and the Supreme Court of Appeal. Pretoria is also a hub for education, with institutions like the University of Pretoria and the Tshwane University of Technology. The city's strong governance and administrative infrastructure have made it an attractive location for businesses, including major financial institutions and multinational corporations. In addition to its economic prowess, Pretoria has made significant contributions towards climate change mitigation, with initiatives like the Pretoria Climate Change Framework and the City of Tshwane's Green Environments Strategy.
