Durban: The Business and Tourism Hub of KwaZulu-Natal

Located on the scenic eastern coast of South Africa, Durban is a thriving metropolis that offers a unique blend of business and leisure. The city is home to the Durban University of Technology and numerous other educational institutions, making it a hub for innovation and entrepreneurship. Durban's business-friendly environment has attracted numerous companies, including major retailers and manufacturers. The city is also a popular tourist destination, with attractions like the Golden Mile, uShaka Marine World, and the Durban Beachfront. In addition to its economic prowess, Durban has made significant contributions towards climate change mitigation, with initiatives like the Durban Climate Change Adaptation Framework and the EThekwini Municipality's Green Building Policy.
