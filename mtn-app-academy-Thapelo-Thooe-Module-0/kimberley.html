Kimberley: The Diamond Capital of South Africa

Welcome to the historic city of Kimberley, known for its rich diamond deposits and vibrant culture. Located in the Northern Cape province, Kimberley has a long history of diamond mining, dating back to the late 19th century. The city is home to the world-famous De Beers diamond mine, which was founded in 1888 and is still in operation today. The mine is owned by De Beers Consolidated Mines, a company that has been at the forefront of the diamond industry for over a century. In addition to its rich diamond deposits, Kimberley is also known for its vibrant arts and culture scene, with attractions like the Kimberley Art Gallery and the Diamond Museum. The city has also made significant contributions towards 4IR, with initiatives like the Kimberley Digital Innovation Hub and the Northern Cape Digital Innovation Centre. With its rich history, vibrant culture, and commitment to innovation, Kimberley is a city that truly shines.
